ARTxFM is a community run, arts-focused radio station based in Louisville, Kentucky. Launched at IdeaFestival in September 2012, the ARTxFM stream is live 24 hours a day, 7 days a week around the globe.

Frequently Asked

100% local

Programming on ARTxFM is 100% local with shows ranging from classical to punk rock to hip hop to country. Our schedule is as diverse as the volunteer DJs who bring it to life.

Support ARTxFM

After years of hard work ARTxFM was recently granted a construction permit by the FCC and will soon begin broadcasting as WXOX 97.1 FM Louisville on the terrestrial dial. We are currently fundraising for this important project and we hope you will join us in the endeavor.
